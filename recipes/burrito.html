Beef Burrito

I chose the beef burrito as it is my second favirte food. I suually fill mine with shredded beef, lettuce, guacomole, pinto beans, rice, and cheese.

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Rice
  • Pinto beans
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Cheese
  • Cilantro
  • Torillas

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a skillet and cook the beef till done. Add seasoning
  2. Cook rice
  3. Place beef in open tortilla on a bed of rice
  4. Add whatever other ingredients you want in the burrito
  5. Wrap it up and enjoy!
