Maplebar recipe

Maplebars

Maplebars are my favorite donut. I could probably eat a dozen of them at a time. Bacon bars are even better!

Ingredients for the donuts

  • 1/2 cup warm water
  • 3/4 cup warm milk
  • 2 tsp dry active yeast
  • 1/2 cup + 1 Tbsp granulated sugar
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 5 eggs
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 5 1/2 cups flour

Ingredients for maple glaze

  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 3 tbsp milk
  • 1 tbsp corn syrup
  • 2 tsp maple extract
  • 2 cups powdered sugar

Instructions

  1. Froth the yeast
  2. Combine everything in a standard mixer
  3. Let the dough rise
  4. Punch and proof a second time
  5. Fry the donuts
  6. Make the maple glaze
  7. Enjoy!
