Pizza recipe

Pepperoni Pizza!

I chose pepperoni pizza for this projext because it is my favorite food. I could eat it everyday!

Ingredients

  • 16 oz pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 20 pepp slices
  • 12 oz mozzerella cheese
  • 1 tsp fresh oregano
  • Flour for rolling dough

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 500 degrees
  2. Roll out the dough with the flour
  3. Add toppings
  4. Cook the pizza for 15 minutesonce oven is done heating up
  5. Slice and enjoy
